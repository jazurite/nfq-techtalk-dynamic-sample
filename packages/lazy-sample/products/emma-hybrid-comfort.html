  • Materials

    Emma smoothly combines high-quality materials to create a dream-like mattress that conforms to every body type. Through the combination of our extensive research and advanced foam technology, Emma offers the comfiest sleep possible. Emma mattresses offer comfort point-elastic caving that enables an ideal and unique fit for your body. As a result, your spine stays straight while at the same time offering you the necessary amount of support and comfort.

    Our top-of-the-line foams are all CertiPUR-US® certified, meaning, they are made without any ozone-depleting chemicals and safe from VOCs, formaldehyde, and phthalates.

Extra strength to provide optimal support

Perfect for all types of sleepers, and activities that need the added reinforcement, the mattress can easily support all body types while maintaining its resilience so that you don’t sink too deep into the mattress.

No more sweaty nights

Designed with a plush and moisture-wicking cover, the Hybrid Comfort is climate-regulating and creates a cool sleep environment. The Airgocell foam allows heat to dissipate away from your body allowing you to fall asleep at the ideal temperature every night.

Undisturbed sleep with zero motion transfer

Made with an open-celled foam that isolates motion, light sleepers can continue to snooze even as their partner tosses and turns in bed. The combined springs in the Hybrid Comfort also help hold the structure of the mattress during movement.

Developed by experts 

The Hybrid Comfort was developed by a team of sleep experts and researchers, bringing top-notch technology to Canada. We know that the quality of your sleep is a long-term investment. Our products are built to last, having been tested and approved in our laboratories.

Emma Hybrid Comfort - Layer by Layer

  • 1

    Breathable and moisture-wicking Top cover

    The Emma cover is soft-to-touch, making it very comfortable to lie on. Built with light fibers, our cover wicks away moisture away and prevents heat from getting trapped on the surface.

  • 2

    The Breezy Airgocell® foam

    Our signature Point Elastic Airgocell technology absorbs and releases excess body heat and moisture, creating an ideal sleeping environment for peaceful sleep.

  • 3

    The cool foam layer that isolates motion

    Find comfort in any sleeping position. Memory foam molds to the unique shape of your body for even pressure distribution and provides great motion isolation so you can sleep undisturbed, even with a fussy partner

  • 4

    The Supportive HRX foam

    Durable HRX foam supports you with just the right level of firmness, without hollows or bumps. Lie back and move freely until you've found your position for a deep sleep.

    The Ergonomic, 7-Zone edge-to-edge pocket springs

    Say goodbye to restless nights! Our individually-wrapped coils are designed to enhance airflow and provide the optimum level of tension and bounce all over the mattress — strong enough to support sleepers of all shape and size. Rest comfortably as our pocket springs adapt to your spine for longer and deeper restorative sleep.

  • 6

    The stabilizing layer

    Long-lasting mattresses need a good foundation. Our base is built to last, providing added stability for decades of quality sleep.

    The resistant, anti-slip, lower cover with handles
